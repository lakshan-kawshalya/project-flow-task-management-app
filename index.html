    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I create a new task?

    Click the "Add Task" button at the top of the dashboard. Fill in the task name, description, priority, and status, then click "Create Task" to add it to your board.

    Can I filter tasks by status?

    Yes! Use the tab bar above the task grid. You can switch between "All", "In Progress", "Completed", and "To Do" to filter your task view instantly.

    How do I sort my tasks?

    Click the "Sort" button next to the Tasks heading. You can sort by date, priority, or name. The sort applies to whichever tab you currently have selected.

    What do the priority badges mean?

    Tasks are color-coded by urgency. High (red) tasks need immediate attention, Medium (yellow) tasks should be tackled soon, and Low (green) tasks can be done when convenient.
